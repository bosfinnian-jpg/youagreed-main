Aims & Objectives

COMM3705 Digital Media Project

AIM

You Agreed.

Is a critical web installation that exposes how digital consent interfaces enable surveillance capitalism by making users experience the gap between clicking "I agree" and understanding what they've actually agreed to.

OBJECTIVES

01
CONSENT ARCHITECTURE
Create a terms of service with strategically buried exhibition clauses that demonstrate how consent interfaces are designed to be ignored rather than read.
02
COUNTDOWN EXPERIENCE
Design a countdown experience that collapses the distance between agreement and consequences, making abstract data extraction visceral and immediate.
03
POST-CONSENT INTERFACE
Build a post-consent choice interface that models what genuine informed consent requires: understanding before agreeing, not buried disclosures.
04
PHYSICAL EXHIBITION
Extend the installation into physical exhibition space, making invisible AI profiling tangible through progressive stations that audiences witness in real-time.
